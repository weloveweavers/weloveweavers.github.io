The 7 Point Plan For Change

Focused priorities that put residents first and rebuild trust in local government.

Honest Government

Run a transparent council, end cronyism, and introduce an Integrity Charter.

More Social Homes

Work with developers and reduce planning delays.

Good Jobs

Create jobs, apprenticeships, and internships through partnerships.

Safer Streets

Tackle crime and anti-social behaviour.

Cleaner Borough

Improve rubbish collection and stop fly-tipping.

Support Small Businesses

Provide grants and ensure fair council contracts.

Education & Opportunity

Expand bursaries, skills training, and university pathways.

Our Community

Weavers Ward is a proud, diverse area with huge potential and clear challenges.

Strengths

  • Strong schools with high achievement
  • Ambitious young people
  • Diverse cultural communities

Challenges

  • Insecure housing
  • Limited access to quality local jobs
  • Lack of transparency in decision-making
