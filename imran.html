Why I Am Standing

Weavers Ward deserves strong, responsible leadership that listens to residents and acts in their best interests. Through my work as a solicitor and community leader, I have seen the real challenges families face and the importance of fair support, safe communities, and opportunities for young people. I am standing because our ward needs a councillor who will represent residents with integrity, speak up for their concerns, and work consistently to improve the wellbeing of our community.

About Imran

Imran Hossain is a solicitor, community leader, and charity founder who has dedicated his career to supporting families, young people, and vulnerable members of the community.

Through his professional work and community initiatives, he has helped many people with legal advice, education, youth development, and charitable projects.

Imran believes in strong communities, fair opportunities, and ensuring that every resident has a voice.

Principal Solicitor – Imran & Co Solicitors
Founder Trustee & CEO – Ummah Appeal
Co-Founder – International Zakat Foundation
Co-Founder – Plumstead Islamic Centre

Imran has led humanitarian programmes and community institutions while providing trusted legal support across immigration, housing, and representation.

Leadership & Public Service — Student Years

During his student years, Imran served as Elected Students’ Officer at the University of Hertfordshire Students’ Union, representing over 27,000 students and organising support programmes. He was Founding Vice-President of the South Asian Students’ Union UK, Vice-President of the Lawyers Without Borders Society, and a campus leader for Amnesty International UK and UNICEF, championing human rights and children’s rights.
